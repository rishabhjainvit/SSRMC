Quality Assurance

At Shri Ram Ready Mix Concrete Pvt. Ltd., quality is at the core of everything we do.
We adhere to stringent quality control measures at every stage
from raw material selection to the final mix delivery—to ensure superior strength, consistency, and durability in our concrete solutions..

We Don’t Guess. We Test.

Our in-house, NABL-standard laboratory ensures that every batch of concrete is tested thoroughly to meet and exceed BIS standards. With advanced equipment and strict quality checks, we guarantee strength, consistency, and reliability in every mix.


Tests Conducted

Cement: Consistency, Setting Time, Compressive Strength - Concrete: Slump, Cube Casting, Compressive Tests (7 & 28 Days) - Aggregates: Sieve Analysis, Absorption, Impact, Crushing Value - Water: pH, TDS - Admixtures: Retention, Gravity, Specific pH.

FAQ

+

How much will it cost?

To get an accurate estimate for your concrete needs, just give us a quick call. We’ll ask a few simple questions about your project size, grade of concrete, delivery location, and any specific mix requirements. Based on that, we’ll provide a ballpark figure. If it suits you, our team can visit your site for a detailed assessment and provide a firm, no-obligation quote.

+

How Long Does Delivery & Supply Take?

The timeline for concrete delivery usually depends on your project requirements, location, and the volume needed. If you have a specific schedule in mind, we’ll do our best to meet it. Most delays typically occur when site readiness or confirmation is pending from the client’s end. With proper coordination, we ensure prompt and hassle-free supply.

+

How do we associate with our Company?

The process starts when you reach out to us with your concrete requirements. Our team reviews your project details and provides suitable recommendations. Based on further discussion, you can choose the grade, quantity, and delivery schedule that fits your needs. Once finalized, we promptly begin batching and ensure timely, quality-assured delivery to your site.

+

Can You Help Improve My Construction Project Quality?

Absolutely! Share your project requirements with us, and our experienced team will guide you with the right concrete mix, technical support, and expert advice. With our quality products and professional service, we help you achieve stronger, more reliable, and efficient construction results.!

+

When do I pay?

For most orders, payments are structured in stages—typically at the time of order confirmation, during supply, and upon completion. However, we’re flexible and can create a payment schedule that suits your project and budget. We aim to make the process convenient and transparent. We accept payments via bank transfer, UPI, and other preferred modes as per client convenience.
